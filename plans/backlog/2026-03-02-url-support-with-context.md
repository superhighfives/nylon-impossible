# URL Support with Context

Add first-class support for URLs as items in Nylon. When a URL is saved, the app should fetch metadata (title, description, site name) and display it as "Read 'The Name of the Article', about XYZ" or similar context.

This complements the share sheet support and makes URLs more useful than just raw links. The app could show previews, favicons, and extract key information to help users remember why they saved something.
